Skardu's natural beauty abounds all around. It's a perfect patchwork of landscapes: desert, mountains, meadows, and forests, all woven together. Visit refreshing lakes that perfectly reflect the lines of the surrounding mountain peaks. Follow babbling streams that run through quaint towns and carve their way through lush green meadows. Hike across the cold desert dunes, with patches of green flora sprouting from the dusty ground.
Behind its breathtaking landscapes, the tales of Skardu's rich history are told throughout the region. Landmarks of Baltistan's history wait to be explored, like Khaplu Palace and Shigar Fort. Once the homes of Skardu's royals, these monuments of a foregone era now house hotels and museums that shine a light on stories of the past.
